# Assembly

Fan units will come in four parts
- Fan Unit board
- Left Bracket
- Right Bracket
- 5V Fan v

:::note
The Fan Unit is designed for a 5V fan like the Noctua [NF-A4x20 5V PWM](https://noctua.at/en/products/fan/nf-a4x20-5v-pwm)
:::

![Fan Unit parts](/img/fan-unit/dumb-parts.png)

Start by installing the Brackets onto the Fan. The Bradis are toolless and wil snap right on

![Two fans with Bkractest installed](/img/fan-unit/brackets.png)

Next intsall the fan into the Fan Unit Baord. 

![Fan installed onto fan unit board](/img/fan-unit/fan.png)

Finaly, connect the Fan to the 4-pin fan header on the fan unit board

![fully assembed Fan Unit](/img/fan-unit/assembled.png)


### Conecting to Compute Blade
:::info
    The Fan Units are designed to work out with the [Blade Runner™️](/blade/blade-runner) enclosers
:::

:::danger
    Pulging the Compute Blade into the Fan Unit incorrectly may lead to permanent damage to the fan unit and the blade
:::

When conecting the blade to the Fan Unit the the borids should be 90° relitive to each other
No addtional work is need to make the Fan unit run.
